About

Opus Classical is a curated catalogue of classical music composers, works and recordings.

How to use

  • Choose a composer: not every composer in history, but not only the most famous ones.
  • Choose a work: not everything that was written, but not only the greatest hits.
  • Choose a recording among several well-respected and newer ones.
  • Click the icon for your streaming app to open the work you're examining.

Supported functionality

  • Mobile and desktop web versions.
  • Direct links to Spotify and Apple Music.
  • Direct links tested on MacOS, Windows and iOS.

Support

Source code is available at Github. Create issues there. Or hit me up on Twitter.
